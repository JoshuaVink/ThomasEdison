Thomas Edison Unbeliever

Thomas Edison was a free thinker and a skeptic. He wasn’t exactly an atheist, but he did think traditional religions were foolish. His beliefs are similar to a diest, but he doesn’t follow any one set of beliefs. Thomas Edison condemned religion in general and even Christianity in specific. About them, he said, "So far as religion of the day is concerned, it is a damned fake... Religion is all bunk... All Bibles are man-made." Thomas Edison believes that young children are indoctrinated into Christianity and are “Incurably religious”. He says that those searching for truth should look to the science of reason and examination instead dogma and darkness, “faith in fiction is a damnable false hope." Thomas Edison was obviously an unbeliever, he said it himself. He was even accused of stealing other inventors’ ideas and taking credit for their inventions. Even though Thomas Edison was an unbeliever, God still used him to bring about great contributions to modern life.
